Cleaning process

Inspection, pre spray, agitation, extraction and fast drying.

We inspect first, apply the right solution, agitate where needed and then carry out a proper deep clean with professional extraction equipment.

  • Inspection for fibre type, stains, wear and traffic lanes.
  • Targeted pre spray to break down deep soil and spots.
  • CRB agitation and hot water extraction on the areas that need it.
  • Drying advice and a final walkthrough before sign off.
