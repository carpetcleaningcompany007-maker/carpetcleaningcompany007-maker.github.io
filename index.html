Professional carpet and upholstery cleaning that is built around real results

The Carpet Cleaning Company provides professional carpet cleaning, upholstery cleaning, rug cleaning, stain removal, certified moth treatment and commercial carpet cleaning across Ludlow, Shrewsbury, Hereford, Worcester and the surrounding towns and villages we cover. We work in family homes, rental properties, holiday lets, offices, pubs, hotels, care settings and other premises where appearance, hygiene and proper results matter.

Our cleaning process is designed for people who want more than a quick rinse over. We begin with inspection and dry soil removal, apply a fibre safe pre treatment chosen for the carpet or upholstery, use counter rotating brush agitation where appropriate and then carry out hot water extraction to remove suspended soil, residues and odours. This helps restore the look and feel of the fibres and can often save carpets, rugs or suites that people assumed needed replacing.
