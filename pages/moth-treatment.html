Moth Treatment for local homes and businesses

Moth activity in carpets and natural fibres can become an expensive problem if it is ignored. We provide carpet moth treatment in Ludlow, Shrewsbury and surrounding areas using licensed insecticides, with inspection, treatment guidance and practical aftercare advice.

This service is especially relevant for wool carpets, rugs, stairs, edges, darker corners, under furniture and quieter rooms where activity is often missed until damage is obvious. If you have seen bare patches, larvae, webbing or repeated carpet damage, it is worth getting the issue looked at properly rather than relying on shop bought sprays.

  • Licensed insecticide treatment for carpet moth activity
  • Suitable for wool carpets, rugs and natural fibre areas
  • Clear advice on affected rooms, repeat risk and aftercare
  • Can be paired with specialist cleaning where appropriate
