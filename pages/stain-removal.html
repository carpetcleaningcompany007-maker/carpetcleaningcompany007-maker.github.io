Stain Removal for local homes and businesses

Not every stain is the same, and not every stain should be treated with the same chemistry. Our stain removal service is built around identifying what the mark is likely to be, what fibre it is sitting in and what treatment stands the best chance of improving it safely. We regularly tackle tea, coffee, tracked in soil, food spills, pet accidents and day to day spots in homes and commercial premises.

Some stains come out fully, some improve significantly and some become permanent because of dye damage or age. We would rather be straight with customers and do the best job possible than over promise. That honest approach is one reason stain removal is often booked together with our carpet cleaning and upholstery cleaning services.

  • Targeted treatment for common household and business stains
  • Assessment of the fibre before chemistry is chosen
  • Can be combined with larger carpet and upholstery jobs
  • Useful for end of tenancy, inspections, guest houses and busy family homes
