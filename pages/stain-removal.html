Professional stain removal

Some stains need more than a normal clean.

Some marks come out with standard cleaning. Others need a much more targeted approach, especially when the stain has set, contains dye, or has been left to soak into the fibre over time.

Professional stain removal is about choosing the right treatment for the stain, the fabric and the overall condition of the item being cleaned. That gives you the best chance of improving or removing the mark without using the wrong chemistry.

We work on carpets, rugs and upholstery across Ludlow and Shrewsbury, helping customers deal with the sort of spills and accidents that ordinary cleaning alone often will not shift.

How stain removal works

A more careful approach gives a better chance of success.

Good stain removal is not about throwing random products at a mark. It starts with understanding what the stain is likely to be, what it is sitting on, and what sort of treatment is suitable for that fibre.

1. Identify the stain as far as possibleThe more we know about the mark, the better the chance of choosing the right treatment.
2. Check the fibre and conditionDifferent fibres react differently, so the carpet, rug or fabric has to be considered first.
3. Apply targeted treatmentThe stain is worked on with a method suited to the type of contamination and the material underneath it.
4. Reassess the resultSome stains improve fully, while others improve partially. We always aim for the best realistic result possible.
