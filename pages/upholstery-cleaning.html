Upholstery Cleaning for local homes and businesses

Upholstery cleaning is one of the fastest ways to improve a room without replacing furniture. We clean sofas, armchairs, dining chairs, fabric headboards and other upholstered seating across Ludlow, Shrewsbury and nearby villages using methods chosen to suit the fabric and the condition of the item.

Whether it is body oils on a favourite sofa, general dullness in a rental property or marked seating in a guest house, we aim to clean thoroughly without over wetting fabrics. This service is often booked alongside carpet cleaning or stain removal so the whole room is finished together.

  • Suitable for domestic and commercial seating
  • Careful assessment of fabric type before cleaning
  • Great for lounges, rental properties, hotels and guest accommodation
  • Can be paired with carpet cleaning and stain removal on the same visit
