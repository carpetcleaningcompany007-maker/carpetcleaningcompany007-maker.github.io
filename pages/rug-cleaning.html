Rug Cleaning for local homes and businesses

Rugs pick up a huge amount of dust, dry soil and spillages, especially in entrance areas, under dining tables and in living rooms. We provide rug cleaning across Ludlow and Shrewsbury with a more careful approach than standard wall to wall carpet work, because rugs often vary more in fibre, backing and dye stability.

From modern area rugs to heavier wool pieces, we inspect first and then choose the safest cleaning route. Many customers add rug cleaning to a wider carpet or upholstery booking so the whole home is cleaned in one visit.

  • Cleaning tailored to the rug material and condition
  • Useful for homes, rentals and guest accommodation
  • Helps lift dry soil, odours and day to day staining
  • Can be booked alongside carpet and upholstery cleaning
