Deep cleaning process

How we clean carpets in Monkmoor

Every carpet is checked before cleaning so the right approach is used for the fibre type, level of soiling and any visible staining.

We apply a professional pre spray to break down soil, then use mechanical agitation with a counter rotating brush where suitable. This helps lift the pile and loosen dirt from both sides of the fibre before hot water extraction flushes the carpet clean.

This process is especially useful for traffic lanes, dull carpets, pet odours, rental properties and rooms that need more than a basic rinse.
