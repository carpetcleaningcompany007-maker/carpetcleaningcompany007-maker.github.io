Our Shrewsbury carpet cleaning process

1. Inspection and quote
We check the carpet type, soiling, stains, access and the level of clean required.
2. Pre vacuum and pre spray
The carpet is prepared and treated with a suitable cleaning solution for the fibre type and soil level.
3. CRB agitation
For deeper cleans, a counter rotating brush helps work the solution through the pile and release trapped dirt.
4. Hot water extraction
Professional extraction equipment rinses and removes loosened soil, cleaning solution and odours.
5. Stain and odour treatment
Where needed, we treat common stains, pet odours, drink spills, traffic lanes and draught marks.
