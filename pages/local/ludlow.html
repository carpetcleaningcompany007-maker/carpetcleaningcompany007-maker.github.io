Professional carpet cleaning in Ludlow

We provide professional carpet cleaning in Ludlow for houses, flats, cottages, rental properties, guest houses, hotels, offices and commercial premises. Whether you need one room cleaned or a full property freshened up, we offer practical packages to suit the job and the level of soiling.

A lot of Ludlow carpet cleaning work involves tracked in soil, food and drink spills, pet issues, traffic marks and general wear in busy homes. We use professional equipment including the Enforcer 400, Airflex, Fusion heat system and power wand setup to deep clean carpets properly, lift out embedded dirt and leave the fibres looking cleaner and fresher.

As well as domestic work, we also carry out cleaning for landlords, estate agents, guest accommodation, hotels and other local businesses. If the job also needs sofa cleaning, rug cleaning, stain removal or licensed moth treatment, we can handle that as one joined up visit rather than sending you elsewhere.
