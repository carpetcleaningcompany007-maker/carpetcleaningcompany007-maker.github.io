Professional carpet cleaning

A cleaner, fresher home starts from the floor up.

Over time, carpets collect dirt, dust, bacteria and allergens deep within the fibres. Hoovering only removes what is sitting on the surface, while everything else stays trapped further down in the pile.

A professional carpet clean goes much further. It helps lift out built up soil, freshen the fibres and restore the overall look and feel of the room. The result is carpets that look brighter, feel softer and leave the home smelling fresher.

It is not just about appearance. A helps improve hygiene, refresh high use areas and bring tired carpets back to life.

How we clean

A from start to finish.

Every carpet is different, so we tailor the clean to suit the material, condition and level of soiling. That helps us get the best possible result for your home or property.

1. Assess the carpetWe look at the fibre, condition, marks and level of soiling so the clean suits the carpet properly.
2. Pre treat and agitateWhere needed, the carpet is treated and worked to loosen dirt and debris from deeper within the fibres.
3. Rinse and extractHot water extraction is used to rinse through the carpet and remove suspended soil for a fresher finish.
4. Final checksWe talk through the finish, drying expectations and any aftercare advice before leaving the room complete.
