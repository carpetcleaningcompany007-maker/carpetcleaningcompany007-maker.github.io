Carpet Cleaning for local homes and businesses

When people search for carpet cleaning in Ludlow or carpet cleaning in Shrewsbury, they usually want more than a quick freshen up. They want a proper deep clean that lifts heavy traffic soil, deals with stains honestly and leaves the carpet looking better, feeling cleaner and drying as quickly as possible. That is exactly what we focus on at The Carpet Cleaning Company.

We work with homeowners, landlords, estate agents, hotels, guest houses and offices. Jobs range from a single room that needs bringing back to life to larger commercial areas that need practical, repeatable results. Our process can include careful vacuuming, targeted pre spray, agitation with a counter rotating brush, hot water extraction and post treatment depending on the carpet type and level of soiling.

  • Deep cleaning using the Enforcer 400 and Airflex
  • Tailored treatment for synthetic and wool carpets
  • Packages available from a quick refresh up to a full deep clean
  • Popular with landlords, estate agents, guest houses, hotels and family homes
