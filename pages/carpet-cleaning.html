Process

How the better cleans are usually carried out.

Each job varies, but the stronger cleaning packages usually follow a structure like this so the customer understands the value of the service.

1. Assess the carpetWe look at the fibre, level of soiling, marks and the result you are aiming for.
2. Pre treat and agitateWhere needed, the carpet is sprayed and worked to loosen embedded soil before extraction.
3. Rinse and extractProfessional hot water extraction removes suspended soil and leaves the carpet far fresher.
4. Final checksWe talk through the finish, likely drying and any aftercare advice for the room.
