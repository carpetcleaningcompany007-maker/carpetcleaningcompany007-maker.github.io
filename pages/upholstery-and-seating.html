Commercial upholstery cleaning

Seating says a lot about the standard of a building.

When customer seating, office chairs or waiting room furniture starts looking tired, it quickly affects the overall impression of the space. Marks, dullness and stale odours can all make a room feel less cared for.

Professional upholstery cleaning helps refresh the fabric, improve presentation and make the furniture more pleasant to use for staff, customers, guests or visitors.

We work with local businesses, hospitality settings, managed spaces and other properties where upholstered seating needs to look clean and presentable.

How we clean

Commercial upholstery cleaned with the setting and fabric in mind.
