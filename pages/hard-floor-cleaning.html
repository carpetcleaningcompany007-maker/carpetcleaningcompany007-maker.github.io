Hard Floor Cleaning for local homes and businesses

Hard floors can still hold a surprising amount of dirt, grease and discolouration, especially in kitchens, entrances, washrooms and commercial walkways. We offer hard floor cleaning across Ludlow and Shrewsbury for properties that need more than a standard mop over.

This service is useful for homes, rentals, offices, guest houses and hospitality settings where the floor needs to look cleaner, brighter and more presentable. It also pairs well with carpet cleaning on mixed floor properties.

  • Ideal for kitchens, entrances, washrooms and communal areas
