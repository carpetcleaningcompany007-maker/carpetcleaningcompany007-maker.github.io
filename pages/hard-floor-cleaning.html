Professional hard floor cleaning

When a hard floor starts looking tired, a can make a huge difference.

Hard floors often seem easy to clean, but once dirt starts building up in the surface texture, joints or grout lines, ordinary mopping usually stops making much difference. The floor may still look dull, patchy or ingrained even after repeated cleaning.

A deeper hard floor clean helps lift out the sort of built up soil that regular maintenance leaves behind. That can improve the appearance of the floor, freshen the room and make the surface look far more presentable again.

We work on domestic and commercial floors across Ludlow and Shrewsbury, helping customers get tired looking hard floors back to a cleaner standard.

How we clean

The right process depends on the floor type.

Different hard floors need different care. The material, finish and level of soiling all matter, so the job is approached with the floor type in mind rather than with a one product fits all method.

1. Assess the floorWe check the floor type, condition, problem areas and how heavily soiled it is.
2. Apply the right cleaning solutionThe floor is treated with a suitable cleaner to help loosen ingrained dirt and residues.
3. Agitate and lift the soilMachine or pad based agitation is used where appropriate to break up the dirt properly.
4. Rinse, extract or finishThe loosened soil is removed and the floor is left cleaner, fresher and more presentable.
