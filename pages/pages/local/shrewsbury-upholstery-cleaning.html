Why professional upholstery cleaning makes a difference

Soft furnishings can hold onto more dirt and odour than many people realise. Armrests, seat cushions and head areas gradually collect body contact, fine dust and residues from everyday living. Over time, this can make upholstery look darker in places, feel less fresh and bring down the overall look of the room.

Professional upholstery cleaning helps remove that build up and improve the overall appearance of the furniture. It can also help freshen the room and bring tired looking seating back to a cleaner, brighter standard.

Using the wrong products on upholstery can cause overwetting, water marks or fabric damage, which is why the method needs to suit the material. That is especially important for mixed fabrics, delicate materials and heavily used seating.
