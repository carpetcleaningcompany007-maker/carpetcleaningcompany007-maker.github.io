Commercial carpet cleaning

Professional cleaning for spaces that need to look right for customers, guests and staff.

Commercial carpets take much heavier use than most household carpets. Entrances, corridors, bedrooms, shared spaces and customer facing areas all pick up dirt quickly, and once traffic lanes start showing, the whole building can feel tired.

Professional commercial carpet cleaning helps improve presentation, freshen the environment and extend the life of the flooring in spaces where appearance matters.

We work with hotels, offices, guest accommodation, estate agents, managed properties and other local businesses across Ludlow and Shrewsbury, arranging the work in a practical way around access, handovers and day to day use.

How we work

Commercial jobs planned around the building, not the other way around.

Every commercial property is different. Room layouts, access times, traffic levels and the type of business all affect how the job should be carried out, so the approach is planned with the site in mind.

1. Discuss the building and requirementsWe establish what areas need doing, what condition they are in and any scheduling needs.
2. Plan access and cleaning approachThe work is arranged in a way that suits the property and the type of commercial use.
3. Clean the carpets properlyThe agreed areas are cleaned using the right level of treatment for the carpets involved.
4. Leave the site practical and presentableWe talk through drying expectations, completed areas and any sensible next steps for maintenance.
