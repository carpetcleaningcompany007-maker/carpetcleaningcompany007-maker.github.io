Professional mattress cleaning

A mattress can hold far more dust and residue than people realise.

Because mattresses are used every night, they naturally collect body oils, sweat, dust and general build up over time. Even when the mattress still looks acceptable on the surface, it can start to feel stale or less fresh than it should.

Professional mattress cleaning helps refresh the surface, reduce built up residues and improve the overall feel of the bed.

This can be useful in family homes, guest rooms, rentals and any property where the condition and presentation of the sleeping area matters.

How we clean

A careful refresh for one of the most important surfaces in the home.

Mattresses need a sensible approach that focuses on cleaning and freshening the surface properly without simply overwetting the bed.

1. Assess the mattressWe look at the condition, any marks, odours and the overall level of soiling.
2. Pre treat where neededAreas with marks or heavier build up can be treated first to help improve the end result.
3. Clean and extract appropriatelyThe mattress is cleaned using a method that helps refresh the surface and remove residues.
4. Explain drying and aftercareWe advise on drying expectations and when the mattress should be ready for use again.
