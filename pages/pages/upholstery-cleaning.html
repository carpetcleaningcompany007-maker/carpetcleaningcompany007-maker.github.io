Professional upholstery cleaning

Bring your furniture back to life with a .

Over time, upholstery collects body oils, dust, marks, spills and everyday grime that gradually makes the fabric look dull and tired. Even when the dirt is not obvious at first glance, it builds up through regular use.

A professional upholstery clean helps refresh the fabric, improve the appearance of the furniture and leave the room feeling fresher overall. Sofas and chairs can often look brighter, smell cleaner and feel far more pleasant once the built up dirt has been properly removed.

Whether it is your main family sofa, occasional chairs, dining chairs or fabric seating in a rental or commercial setting, the aim is always the same: a cleaner, fresher and more presentable finish.

How we clean

A careful clean that suits the fabric.

Not all upholstery should be treated in exactly the same way. The fabric, condition and level of soiling all matter, which is why the clean is tailored to the furniture rather than rushed through with a one size fits all approach.

1. Assess the fabricWe check the material, condition and any obvious marks or problem areas before cleaning begins.
2. Pre treat where neededAreas of build up or marking can be treated first to help loosen soil and improve the final result.
3. Clean and extractThe upholstery is cleaned using methods suited to the fabric so dirt and residues can be removed properly.
4. Final checksWe go through the finish with you and explain drying expectations and any aftercare advice needed.
