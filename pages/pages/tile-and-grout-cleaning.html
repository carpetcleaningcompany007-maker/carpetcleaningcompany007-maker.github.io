Professional tile and grout cleaning

When grout lines darken, the whole floor can look tired.

Tile floors often lose their clean appearance gradually. Dirt settles into the grout, surface texture and edges until the whole floor starts looking dull, patchy or harder to maintain.

Professional tile and grout cleaning helps lift that ingrained dirt properly so the floor can look brighter, cleaner and more presentable again.

This is useful in kitchens, bathrooms, hallways, communal areas and commercial properties where tiled floors need a deeper clean than normal household mopping can usually provide.

How we clean

A deeper clean aimed at the places dirt collects most.

The main problem with tiled floors is usually not the flat tile itself but the textured areas, joints and edges where dirt has built up over time. That is where the right process makes the biggest difference.

1. Assess the tiled areaWe look at the tile type, grout condition and level of ingrained soiling.
2. Apply suitable cleanerThe floor is treated to loosen the grime sitting in the grout lines and surface texture.
3. Agitate and clean thoroughlyMachine or pad based cleaning is used where suitable to break the dirt up properly.
4. Remove residues and finishThe loosened soil is taken away so the floor is left cleaner and looking much fresher.
