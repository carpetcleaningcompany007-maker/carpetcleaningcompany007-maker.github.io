Typical guide

How we usually price domestic carpet cleaning.

For many homes, the first carpet is priced at £75 and additional carpets are usually £45 each on the Deluxe Deep Clean service. Hall, stairs and landing may be priced together depending on layout and size. Rugs, upholstery, stain work and commercial spaces are quoted separately.

The fastest way to get an accurate price is to send a few photos on WhatsApp with the rooms you want cleaned.

What affects the final price?

Prices can vary depending on carpet size, level of soiling, access, stain treatment needed, furniture movement, fibre type and whether you also want rugs or upholstery cleaned at the same time.
