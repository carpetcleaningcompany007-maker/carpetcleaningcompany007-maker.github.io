How room pricing works

Exact pricing depends on room size, fibre type, access, condition and how many areas are being cleaned. Multi room jobs usually price better than a single small visit.

Stairs and landings

Hall, stairs and landing can usually be priced together depending on layout. Send a few photos and we can guide you quickly.

Commercial and larger jobs

Hotels, offices, rentals, care settings and larger floor areas are quoted separately so the method and timing fit the job properly.
