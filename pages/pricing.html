Why the prices are different

Going cheaper is not always the best option, but we offer it anyway.

If you can’t beat them, join them. We used to lose customers to lower prices from other companies, so we now offer a lower priced option as well. The difference is that we tell you clearly what you are getting.

The Light Clean and the Deep Clean are not the same process. They do not use the same setup. You are not only hiring me, you are hiring the equipment as well, and the equipment used on the Deep Clean is far more advanced.

That is why the Deep Clean reaches further into the carpet, removes more embedded soil, improves the feel underfoot, and gives a more complete result overall.
