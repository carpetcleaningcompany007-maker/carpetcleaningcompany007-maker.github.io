Why prices vary

The cheapest clean and the best clean are not the same service.

The Refresh Clean is there for customers who want the most affordable option and understand that it is a lighter maintenance style clean. It is ideal where the carpets only need a simple freshen up.

The Deluxe Deep Clean is our proper professional clean. It involves more preparation, more product knowledge, more physical work, and a much better end result. The difference in price is often smaller than people expect, but the difference in quality is huge.

Please note: We do not offer the budget Refresh Clean for end of tenancy work. If the result needs to stand up to a landlord or agent inspection, the deeper professional option is the right route.

A real example

Why choosing the cheapest quote can cost more.

A tenant once chose a cheaper internet quote instead of the proper professional clean. The company rushed the job, the carpets did not look noticeably better, and there was no proper paperwork. The landlord would not accept it and would not release the deposit.

We then had to go back in and clean the carpets properly with the deep clean option. In the end, the customer paid twice. It worked out well for us because we now clean for the landlord as well, but the customer would have been far better off choosing the right service the first time.
