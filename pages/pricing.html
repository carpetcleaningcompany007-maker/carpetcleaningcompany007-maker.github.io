Why the prices are different

Going cheaper is not always the best option, but we offer it anyway. If you can’t beat them, join them.

There are many companies on the internet offering three rooms for £99, or around £29.99 to £30 per room. Many customers are simply unaware that there are major differences between the cleans being offered.

Sometimes companies advertising these types of prices will often try to upsell once they get to the door. We will not do that. That is why we are telling you now in advance exactly what you are paying for, so everything is clear before we arrive.
