Why the prices are different

Going cheaper is not always the best option, but we offer it anyway.

We used to lose customers to cheaper quotes until we decided to offer that option as well.

That is exactly why the Light Clean is there. Some customers are shopping mainly on price, so we give them that option as well.

The difference is that we are completely upfront about what you get. The cheaper option uses a lighter setup. The Deep Clean uses different equipment, more heat, more agitation, more time, and a far more complete process.

With us:
  • We will not add extra costs when we arrive
  • We use professional equipment, not shop bought machines
  • All prices are agreed before we start
  • We are fully insured
  • If you’re not happy, you don’t pay
Before choosing any company, including us, ask these questions:
  • What equipment are you using?
  • Is the price fixed?
  • Will I get an invoice?
  • Are you insured?
  • Will you guarantee the work?
Important stain note: No cleaner can guarantee to remove every single stain from every carpet. Some stains such as red wine, makeup, dye stains, and other specialist marks may need advanced stain removal at extra cost.
