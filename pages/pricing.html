Why the prices are different

Going cheaper is not always the best option, but we offer it anyway.

If you cannot beat them, join them. That is exactly why the Light Clean is there. Some customers are shopping mainly on price, so we give them that option as well.

The difference is that we are upfront about what is included. The cheaper option uses a lighter setup. The Deep Clean uses different equipment, more heat, more agitation, more time, and a far more complete process.

That way you can choose what matters more to you. The cheapest possible quote, or the best possible job.

With us:
  • We will not ask for extra money when we turn up
