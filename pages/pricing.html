Why the prices are different

Going cheaper is not always the best option, but we offer it anyway.

If you can’t beat them, join them. The low priced option is there for customers who want the cheapest possible quote and understand that it is a lighter clean with a more basic process.

The Deep Clean is very different. It uses our top tier setup, more heat, more agitation, more time and a more complete process. That is why it reaches deeper into the carpet and gives a noticeably better result.

Adding protection helps that result last longer. It puts a protective coating around the fibres so everyday dirt and spills are easier to deal with and regular vacuuming becomes more effective.

Simple truth: the cheaper option is there if you want it. But when carpets need proper work, the Deep Clean gives you more value, a better finish and a more complete result.

Protection explained

Why customers add fibre protection after a deep clean.

Once your carpet or upholstery has been cleaned properly, applying protection helps keep it that way. The Teflon polycarbonate treatment coats the fibres and creates a slippery barrier that helps repel dry soil and slows down re soiling.

Think of it like the non stick effect on a frying pan. Dirt has a harder time sticking to the fibres, routine vacuuming becomes more effective, and the clean look lasts longer.
