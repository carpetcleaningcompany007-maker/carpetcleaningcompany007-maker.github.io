Professional carpet and upholstery cleaning in Ludlow and Shrewsbury

With around 15 years in the wider cleaning industry and around 10 years specialising in carpet and upholstery cleaning, we provide a professional service for homes, hotels, guest houses, offices, estate agents and managed properties across Ludlow, Shrewsbury and surrounding towns.

We invest in professional equipment including the Enforcer 400, Airflex, Fusion heat system and power wand set up so we can deliver proper deep cleaning rather than a quick surface rinse. Alongside carpet cleaning, we provide upholstery cleaning, stain removal, rug cleaning, hard floor cleaning and licensed moth treatment.

Whether the job is a single room, a full house clean, a sofa clean, guest accommodation turnaround work or ongoing commercial maintenance, we tailor the service to the condition of the carpets and the finish you want to achieve.
