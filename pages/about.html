Experienced local carpet cleaning backed by professional equipment

We have around 15 years in the wider cleaning industry and around 10 years specialising in carpet and upholstery cleaning. Over that time we have invested in professional equipment including the Enforcer 400, Airflex, Fusion heat system and power wand setup so we can offer more than a cheap splash and dash clean.

We clean carpets, upholstery, rugs and hard floors, carry out targeted stain removal and provide licensed moth treatment. We also work with hotels, guest houses, estate agents and office clients, so we are used to jobs where presentation matters and timing matters.

We are proud to cover Ludlow, Shrewsbury and surrounding towns, helping homeowners, landlords, hotels, offices and estate agents get the right clean for the job with straightforward quoting and dependable service.
