A local company built around practical cleaning work

The Carpet Cleaning Company is based in Ludlow and serves homes and businesses across South Shropshire and Shrewsbury. The aim is simple: deliver a better clean, use the right equipment, and be straight with customers about what their carpets, upholstery, rugs or floors actually need.

We work across domestic and commercial settings, including family homes, rentals, estate agent work, hotels, guest houses and offices. Our equipment includes the Enforcer 400, Airflex and Fusion heat system, which gives us the flexibility to build packages around the property and the job rather than trying to force every booking into the same method.

Moth treatment is also part of the service range, using licensed insecticides and a more specialist approach than a general cleaning visit. That makes the site more useful for customers searching for real local services, not just broad generic wording.

  • Carpet cleaning, upholstery cleaning and stain removal
  • Rug cleaning and hard floor cleaning
  • Licensed carpet moth treatment
  • Hotel, office, estate agent and managed property work
